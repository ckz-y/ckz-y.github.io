Master of Science in Advanced Computer Science candidate at the University of Oxford.

I recently graduated Cum Laude from Princeton University with a Bachelor of Science in Engineering in Operations Research and Financial Engineering and certificates in Statistics and Machine Learning and Computer Science. My senior thesis research, conducted under the supervision of Dr. Ioannis Akrotirianakis, investigated equitable urban cycling network expansion using mathematical optimization.
