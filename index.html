Coleman Yanagisawa

Masters of Science in Advanced Computer Science Candidate at the University of Oxford.

I recently graduated with Honors from Princeton University with a Bachelor's of Science in Engineering in Operations Research and Financial Engineering and certificates in Statistics and Machine Learning and Computer Science. My senior thesis research, conducted with Dr. Ioannis Akrotirianakis, investigated equitable urban cycling network expansion using mathematical optimization. During my time at Princeton, I also interned at IBM's Chief Analytics Office and at Amtrak.

I am broadly interested in the applications of data for social good.
